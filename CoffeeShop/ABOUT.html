ABOUT THE CAFE

The Black Cafe first opened its doors in July of 2008. The founder, Haden Bauer, wanted a coffee shop that offered a cool, laid-back aesthetic combined with the best damn coffee out there.


Unfortunately, this kind of establishment didn't exist, so he decided to open one of his own. Located in the heart of Brooklyn, New York, The Black Cafe focuses on specialty drinks brewed from only the finest Brazilian coffee beans.


In addition to some amazing coffee, The Black Cafe also offers a wide range of vegan food options, so everyone can find something great to eat within their personal dietary needs.

ABOUT THE OWNER

Haden Bauer was born in Southlake, Texas and moved around a lot growing up. He lived in New York, Illinois, California, and Ohio before settling down in Brooklyn, New York.


Growing up in cities with very different cultures and aesthetics inspired him to create a new place with a cool atmosphere for anyone to hang out, socialize, and drink high quality coffee and tea.


Haden has a full-service restaurant in the works that is set to open in Williamsburg, New York in 2020.
